About Us

VenueBooking is your one-stop solution for discovering and booking the perfect wedding venues across India. With a handpicked selection of luxurious banquet halls, resorts, and destination spots, we help make your big day truly special.

Our platform simplifies the search process by offering detailed listings, verified reviews, and the ability to compare venues based on your preferences. Whether you’re planning an intimate gathering or a grand celebration, we have options to suit every style and budget.

Founded in 2020, VenueBooking has helped over 10,000 couples find their dream wedding location. Our mission is to bring transparency, convenience, and joy to your venue booking journey.

We partner with venue owners and event planners to ensure availability, accurate pricing, and great service — giving you peace of mind when it matters most.
